AI Revolution: How Artificial Intelligence is Changing Our World

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become a major part of modern life. From voice assistants to self-driving cars, AI technologies are shaping how we live, work, and interact with machines.

Applications of AI

Healthcare

In healthcare, AI helps doctors diagnose diseases faster and more accurately. AI-powered systems can analyze X-rays and MRI scans to detect early signs of illness.

Transportation

Self-driving cars are one of the most famous examples of AI in action. These vehicles use sensors and algorithms to navigate roads safely.
